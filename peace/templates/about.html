About Kenya Digital Peace Platform

The Kenya Digital Peace Platform (KDPP) is a national digital initiative designed to empower citizens to report conflicts, prevent violence, and support peacebuilding organizations across Kenya.

Our Mission

Empowering citizens to report conflicts, promote unity, and safeguard communities.

Our Vision

A peaceful, united Kenya where technology enables every citizen to protect their community.

What KDPP Does

  • Provides a safe and anonymous space for reporting conflicts.
  • Detects early warning signs of violence using real-time data.
  • Connects citizens with peace and security organizations.
  • Maps hotspots to help authorities respond faster.
