About Kenya Digital Peace Platform

Promoting peace, unity, and conflict prevention across Kenya.

Our Mission

Kenya Digital Peace Platform (KDPP) is a national digital initiative designed to empower citizens to report conflicts, prevent violence, and support peacebuilding organizations across the country.

What KDPP Does

✔ Provides a safe and anonymous space for conflict reporting.

✔ Detects early warning signs of violence through real-time data.

✔ Connects citizens with peace and security organizations.

✔ Maps hotspots to help leaders respond faster.

Why KDPP Matters

Kenya has a long history of peace but also moments of conflict. KDPP helps communities stay safe by ensuring every voice is heard, every incident is documented, and action is taken quickly.

Our Vision

A peaceful, united Kenya where technology empowers citizens to protect their communities.
